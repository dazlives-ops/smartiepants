About Our Centre

Smartie Pants is a privately owned and operated long daycare and pre-school in Diamond Creek. We offer a play-based learning environment immersed in intentional teaching practices to help children be everything they can be. Our proudly long-standing team offers higher educator-to-child ratios in a beautiful one acre natural setting that promotes exploration, adventure and imaginative play experiences. We look forward to welcoming your family to our family.
